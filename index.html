Features

1. Traffic & Controlls

Navigate through the bustling lanes of Retronika's futuristic cityscape, where every moment is a pulse-pounding race against time. Feel the exhilaration as you push the limits of speed, darting through the six-lane traffic with lightning reflexes.

But heed this warning, adventurer: in a world where flying jet cars reign supreme, danger lurks around every corner. High-speed thrills come hand in hand with peril, demanding utmost caution as you weave through the urban labyrinth.

2. Jet Bike

In the electrifying realm of Retronika, your brand new jet bike is your lifeline amidst the chaos. Feel its power surge beneath you as you navigate the neon-lit streets, each twist of the throttle a daring dance with destiny. But don't overlook the wealth of knowledge waiting for you on the dashboard. Every flicker of light, every gauge, holds vital information to guide you through the labyrinthine cityscape.

Amidst the rush of adrenaline, remember the importance of balance. While speed may be exhilarating, the brake pedal is your steadfast ally, ensuring control and precision in the face of danger. Soar through the skies, adventurer, but never forget: in Retronika, survival depends on mastering the delicate art of speed and caution.

3. Weapons

In Retronika's relentless battleground, wield your firearms with cunning precision. With two hands at your disposal, adapt to each scenario with finesse. Choose the pistol for distant engagements, its accuracy unmatched. And when foes close in, let the shotgun's thunderous blast clear the path to victory. Master this deadly dance of versatility, and conquer the chaos that unfolds before you.

4. Drones

Beware, brave adventurer, for danger lurks on the horizon. Drones swarm the skies, relentless in their pursuit. Some lag behind, sluggish in their chase—simply outpace them and leave them in your wake. But beware the swift ones, for they match your speed with relentless fervor. In their shadow, your trusty gun becomes your shield, your salvation.

Yet, amidst the chaos, a greater threat looms. When the boss descends, time becomes your greatest adversary. There is no room for hesitation, no margin for error. Steel yourself, for in this pivotal moment, survival hinges on split-second decisions and unwavering resolve. Embrace the challenge, for in the crucible of conflict, true heroes are forged.

A bit deeper

Intro

Step into a world where the future unfolds like a vivid dream, a symphony of neon-lit streets and soaring skyscrapers. But hold on tight, for in Retronika, tomorrow has taken a different turn. Here, the future is not a distant horizon, but a pulsating reality, beckoning you to dive headfirst into the realms of retrofuturism.

Feel the rush as you commandeer your jet bike, weaving through sky traffic with effortless grace. Each twist and turn is a dance with destiny, a testament to your skill and daring. And when adversaries loom on the horizon, fear not, for cyber-guns await at your fingertips. With a steady hand and a sharp eye, you'll outmaneuver your foes, leaving them in your wake.

Gameplay

Embark on a captivating journey through Retronika's intricately woven narrative, where each mission is a tapestry of diverse gameplay modes: checkpoints, heart-pounding chases, and battles against energy starvation. Your triumphs hinge not only on raw talent but also on the strategic utilization of upgrades for your bike and weaponry.With each mission comes the promise of replayability, inviting you to delve deeper into the neon-lit metropolis and unearth hidden resources and enhancements. Your choices shape the outcome, as you navigate through a maze of challenges and opportunities.Expect to lose yourself in the pulsating rhythm of Retronika for 6 to 12 hours, each moment filled with adrenaline-fueled excitement and the thrill of discovery. Are you ready to write your own destiny in the annals of retrofuturism?

Core

Enter a world where the essence of the game lies in navigating through a procedurally-generated city amidst bustling traffic. Control a motorcycle-like jet-bike, deftly maneuvering vertically and horizontally through the urban labyrinth.While the traffic mirrors real-life scenarios, a vertical dimension adds an exhilarating twist, forming a 3 x 3 grid of vehicles. This dynamic setup creates countless variations and riveting encounters.Armed with weapons, players can shoot down chasers and obstacles, adding to the adrenaline-fueled thrill of the chase. Prepare for an adventure where split-second decisions determine survival in a world brimming with danger and excitement.
